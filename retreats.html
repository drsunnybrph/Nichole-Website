— The Vision

A gathering that feeds more than the body

Madre y Agua retreats are immersive, multi-day experiences that bring all four offerings into one container — herbal medicine, ancestral food, ritual arts, and the space to learn together.

They are designed for people who want to step out of ordinary time for a few days and into something slower, more intentional, and more deeply nourishing. Small groups. Real food. Plants. Ceremony. Rest.

Retreats are currently in development. Join the waitlist to be the first to know when dates are announced.

Small & Intimate

Maximum 10–12 participants. Enough space for real conversation and real rest.

Food at the Center

Every meal is prepared with intention — seasonal, medicinal, and deeply nourishing.

Rooted in Place

Each retreat is shaped by the land it happens on — its plants, its seasons, its character.

Ceremony & Integration

Daily ritual and transition practices that help the experience land in the body, not just the mind.

— What to Expect

A day might include

↑

Morning

A slow opening practice — breathwork, herbal tea, somatic movement, or time in stillness before the day begins.

◯

Midday

A workshop or guided experience — plant identification, herbal formulation, medicinal cooking, or cyclical practice.

↓

Evening

A communal meal prepared with seasonal ingredients, followed by ceremony, sharing, or free time to integrate.
