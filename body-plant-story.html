Private Sessions

Body, Plant
& Story

These sessions are an invitation to return to your body's own rhythms.

— The Work

Listening for what is asking to be tended.

Together, we explore the places where life may be asking for attention — through the body, the emotions, relationships, creativity, fertility, grief, transition, or the season of life you find yourself in.

Through conversation, observation, herbal support, and practical ritual, we begin listening for the relationships that are asking to be tended.

This work is collaborative, relational, and rooted in curiosity. A space to listen, reflect, and cultivate a deeper relationship with yourself and the life you are living.

— The Approach

Just as we would not ask the desert to become a rainforest, we honor the landscape of your body as it is.

Complex, intelligent, adaptive, and worthy of care.

— Each Session Includes
Consultation

A comprehensive consultation

Unhurried conversation and observation — listening together for the relationships asking to be tended.

Formulation

A custom herbal formulation

Herbal support prepared specifically for your body and the season of life you are in.

Practice

A personalized practice

Simple, practical ritual to support integration into daily life.

Begin the conversation.

Sessions are shaped by the person and the season. Reach out to share where you are, and we'll find the right beginning.
