— What This Is

Healing through relationship with the plant world

Herbal medicine at Madre y Agua is not a prescription — it is a conversation. Between your body, its patterns, and the plants that have long held knowledge of how to restore balance.

Each consultation is a space to be heard and to reconnect with your body's innate intelligence. Custom formulations are drawn from ancestral traditions, lived practice, and a deep personal relationship with the plants themselves.

— Common Questions

Frequently Asked

Do I need prior experience with herbal medicine?

Not at all. This work meets you exactly where you are — whether you're brand new to plants or have been working with them for years.

Are consultations in-person or virtual?

Both options are available. Virtual consultations are held via video call. Formulations can be shipped or arranged for local pickup.

Can this work alongside conventional medicine?

Yes. Herbal medicine is a complement to, not a replacement for, medical care. We'll always discuss any medications or conditions in your intake.

How long until I notice results?

Herbs work with the body's own rhythms — most people notice shifts within 2–4 weeks, with deeper changes emerging over 1–3 months of consistent practice.
