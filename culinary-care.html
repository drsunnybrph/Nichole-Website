— What This Is

The kitchen as a place of healing and memory

Ancestral culinary care returns food to its original purpose — not fuel, but medicine. Not convenience, but ceremony. Each meal is crafted with knowledge of the land, the season, and the specific needs of the people it nourishes.

Whether for a private event, an intimate retreat, or ongoing personal chef services, the food that comes from this work is rooted, real, and deeply human.

This offering includes

  • Private chef services (events & retreats)
  • Ongoing personal chef arrangements
  • Custom seasonal menu development
  • Nutritional & medicinal food planning
  • Kitchen consultations & pantry guidance

— Common Questions

Frequently Asked

What types of cuisine or traditions do you work with?

The cooking draws primarily from Mesoamerican, Latin American, and indigenous plant-based traditions, while adapting to the specific cultural roots and needs of each client.

Do you accommodate dietary restrictions?

Yes. Every menu is built around your specific needs — whether that includes allergies, intolerances, or intentional dietary practices.

How far in advance should I book for an event?

For retreats and larger events, 4–6 weeks in advance is ideal. For personal chef services, 2 weeks notice is generally sufficient.

Do you travel for retreats?

Yes, travel is available for retreats and extended engagements. Please reach out to discuss logistics and availability.
