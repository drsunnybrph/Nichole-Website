— What This Is

Knowledge that returns wisdom to where it belongs — with the people

Educational spaces at Madre y Agua are built on one belief: the knowledge of how to care for yourself and your community should be accessible, embodied, and practical — not locked behind credentials or cost.

Workshops, writings, and future courses offer tangible frameworks you can bring into your kitchen, your body, your daily life — drawing from the deep wells of ancestral practice.

This offering includes

  • In-person & virtual workshops
  • Writings & guides (free & paid)
  • Seasonal care curricula
  • Future self-paced courses
  • Community learning circles
— What We Teach

Areas of Learning

01

Herbal Foundations

An introduction to working with medicinal plants — identification, preparation, and building a home apothecary from scratch.

02

Nourishing the Cycle

Food and herbs for each phase of the menstrual cycle — practical, seasonal, and grounded in ancestral food wisdom.

03

The Home Apothecary

Building a living medicine cabinet with tinctures, teas, salves, and oxymels you can make yourself at home.

04

Seasonal Living

How to eat, rest, move, and care for the body in alignment with spring, summer, autumn, and winter.

05

Postpartum & New Life

Traditional practices for the fourth trimester — nourishment, rest, ritual, and community care after birth.

06

Writing & Deep Reflection

Essays and guided writings exploring the intersection of body, land, and daily practice as acts of remembrance.

— Common Questions

Frequently Asked

Do I need any prior knowledge to attend a workshop?

No. All workshops are designed to be accessible regardless of prior experience. Beginners are warmly welcomed.

How can I stay informed about upcoming workshops?

Reach out via email to be added to the mailing list. Upcoming offerings are shared there first.

Are workshops offered virtually?

Many workshops are offered both in-person and virtually. The format is noted when each workshop is announced.

Can organizations or schools book workshops?

Yes. Custom workshops can be arranged for organizations, schools, wellness spaces, and community groups. Reach out to discuss.
